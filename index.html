Introduction To Our Markaz

Our organization serves as a center of Islamic learning, spiritual growth and community development. Through educational institutions, welfare projects and guidance programs, we strive to strengthen faith and character in society.

For generations, our scholars and leaders have worked tirelessly to spread the message of peace, knowledge and service while maintaining the rich traditions of Islamic scholarship.
